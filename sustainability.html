Our Commitment to Sustainability

Fashion with a conscience

At Atelier Noir, we believe luxury and sustainability go hand in hand. Every decision we make considers the impact on our planet and the people who make our clothing.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner only with suppliers who meet our strict ethical standards. Every material is traced from source to finished product, ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions throughout our supply chain.

Quality Over Quantity

Each piece is designed to last for years, not seasons. By creating timeless designs with exceptional quality, we reduce waste and promote conscious consumption. Our goal is for you to wear our pieces for a lifetime.

Sustainable Materials

We prioritize natural, organic, and recycled materials. 80% of our collection uses sustainable fabrics including organic cotton, recycled wool, and responsibly sourced silk. We're constantly researching and adopting new sustainable materials.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

All our shipments are carbon neutral. We offset 100% of shipping emissions through verified environmental projects focused on reforestation and renewable energy.

Circular Fashion

We offer a take-back program for worn Atelier Noir pieces. Items are either resold through our vintage program or recycled into new materials, keeping textiles out of landfills.

Giving Back

1% of all sales goes to environmental conservation organizations focused on protecting natural ecosystems and combating climate change. Since 2020, we've donated over $500,000 to these causes.

Our Goals for 2026

  • ✓ 100% sustainable materials by end of 2026
  • ✓ Zero waste packaging
  • ✓ B Corp certification
  • ✓ Living wage guarantee for all workers
