Shipping Information

Fast, secure delivery to your door

Free Standard Shipping

Enjoy complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $200. Delivery within 5-7 business days.

Express Shipping

Need it sooner? Choose express shipping at checkout for delivery within 2-3 business days. $25 flat rate.

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates calculated at checkout. Delivery times vary by destination.

Shipping Process

  • Order Processing: Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM ship the next business day.
  • Tracking: You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
  • Signature Required: For security, orders over $500 require signature upon delivery.
  • Packaging: All items are carefully packaged in our signature boxes with tissue paper and branded materials.
